Introduction

Welcome to AG | Home ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What information we collect
  • How we use and protect that information
  • Your rights regarding your personal data

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using AG | Home, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account or use our services, you may provide:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and support communication
  • Phone Number: For phone-based authentication (optional)
  • Display Name: Profile information you choose to provide
  • Profile Picture: From third-party authentication providers
  • Password: Securely encrypted and never stored in plain text
  • Passwords Stored: Encrypted data of passwords you save in our system

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, browser type, operating system
  • Log Data: Access times, pages viewed, referrer information
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (not precise)
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with our services

Information from Third Parties

When you use social authentication (Google, GitHub, Facebook, Yahoo), we receive:

  • Email address
  • Display name / profile name
  • Profile picture URL
  • Any scopes you authorize

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for:

  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining your account
  • Service Delivery: Providing password management and security features
  • Authentication: Verifying your identity securely
  • Communication: Sending important updates and support responses
  • Security: Detecting fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve our services
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory obligations
  • Customer Support: Responding to your questions and concerns

Authentication Methods & Data

AG | Home supports multiple authentication methods. Each method has different data requirements:

Email & Password

Data Collected: Email address, password (hashed)

Storage: Firebase Authentication database

Privacy: Passwords are never stored in plain text. Hashing is performed by Firebase with industry-standard algorithms.

Google Authentication

Data Collected: Email, name, profile picture

Provider: Google (Alphabet Inc.)

Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

Credentials: Handled directly by Google, never shared with our servers

GitHub Authentication

Data Collected: Email, username, profile picture, public profile

Provider: GitHub (Microsoft Corporation)

Privacy Policy: GitHub Privacy Policy

Scopes Requested: User email access

Facebook Authentication

Data Collected: Email, name, profile picture, public profile

Provider: Meta Platforms, Inc.

Privacy Policy: Facebook Privacy Policy

Scopes Requested: Public profile, email address

Yahoo Authentication

Data Collected: Email, name, profile information

Provider: Yahoo (Verizon Media)

Privacy Policy: Yahoo Privacy Policy

Scopes Requested: Profile, email

Phone Number Authentication

Data Collected: Phone number, SMS verification code

Storage: Firebase Authentication with reCAPTCHA protection

SMS Provider: Firebase uses trusted SMS providers for delivery

Privacy: Verification codes expire within 15 minutes and are single-use

Guest/Anonymous Authentication

Data Collected: Unique anonymous identifier only

Storage: Firebase Authentication

Privacy: No personal information is collected

Duration: Session-based; data cleared on logout

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

Encryption

  • In Transit: All data is transmitted via HTTPS/TLS 1.2+
  • At Rest: Sensitive data is encrypted using AES-256
  • Password Storage: Passwords are hashed with bcrypt or equivalent
  • Stored Passwords: Your saved passwords are encrypted with your master password

Infrastructure Security

  • Google Cloud Firebase infrastructure with automatic scaling and backups
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Intrusion detection and prevention systems
  • DDoS protection and rate limiting
  • Access logs and monitoring

Authentication Security

  • Multi-factor authentication support via SMS
  • reCAPTCHA protection against automated attacks
  • Session management with automatic timeouts
  • Secure token generation and validation
Important Note: While we implement industry-standard security practices, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We use them to enhance your experience and secure your account.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication and security (session tokens)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your theme (dark/light) and language settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use the platform (optional)

Local Storage

We use browser local storage to:

  • Store your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Remember language selection
  • Store session identifiers for authentication

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
Note: Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from logging in or using the service properly.
